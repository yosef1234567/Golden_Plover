Golden plovers are remarkable birds. During the summer, they inhabit the far Arctic regions. However, as autumn approaches, they depart from their nesting areas and embark on a southward migration worldwide.

This Pacific Golden Plover is particularly inspiring. Since 2017 and every year thereafter (up to 2024), it has returned to the same spot on Tel Baruch Beach in Tel Aviv. Remarkably, it even perches on the same rock as it did a year ago.
